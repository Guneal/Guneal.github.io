Policies

Refund and Dispute Policy

Refunds are not available for digital products (e.g., logos and digital assets) as they cannot be returned once delivered and approved by the client. I prioritize revising and iterating on the asset until it meets your satisfaction, at which point it is considered complete. If changes are requested after final approval, additional payment will be required for further adjustments.

Cancellation Policy

Projects can be cancelled by either party with 48 hours’ notice before work begins. If I’ve started work, a cancellation fee of 50% of the agreed rate applies to cover time invested. Full payment is due if work is complete.

Terms

All work is commissioned under a freelance agreement. Payment is due within 7 days of project completion via Stripe. Late payments incur a 5% fee per week.
